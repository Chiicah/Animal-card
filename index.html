About Ruby

Ruby is our little sweetheart of just 5 years old. She spent the early years of her life as a victim of the dog meat trade. We found her in a cage filled with other dogs in unimaginable conditions and thought her dead. We had the means only to adopt one dog and Ruby already spoke to our hearts. There was no way we could leave her there to continue in such inhumane and unimaginable life. We adopted her and she's been part of our family since then.
They're however lots of other dogs that could be saved today with your help. Adopt one dog and save a life!
